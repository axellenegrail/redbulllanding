History

Red Bull was founded in 1987 by Dietrich Mateschitz and Chaleo Yoovidhya. The brand was inspired by a Thai energy drink and quickly became successful around the world. Red Bull is known not only for its energy drinks, but also for its strong presence in sports, music, and extreme events.
